Prince of Persia (2008) system requirements:
 Radeon X800 GTO graphics card with a Core 2 Duo E4500 
2.2GHz or Athlon 64 X2 Dual Core 4400+ processor to reach the recommended specs, achieving high graphics setting on 1080p.
 The RAM requirements are at least a 2 GB memory. You will require a DirectX 9 GPU.
